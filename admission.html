Admissions Process

Joining the Enfance Family is Simple!

We strive to make the admission journey smooth, transparent, and welcoming. Follow these four basic steps to secure your child's enrollment:

Fill the Online Enquiry Form: Complete the form on the right with child details and program preferences.
Personal Counsellor Call: Our counselor will call you within 24 hours to clarify curriculum details, fee structures, and schedules.
Campus Tour & Child Visit: Visit the school with your child to meet classroom teachers and inspect facility safety.
Documents & Fee Finalization: Submit copy of birth certificate, passport photos, medical history, and complete booking formalities.

Request a Call Back

Fill details and our counselor will coordinate a school tour for you.
