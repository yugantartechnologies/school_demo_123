About Our Preschool

Know more about our vision, learning philosophy, facilities, and loving educators.

Welcome to Enfance

Nurturing happy minds & strong characters.

We believe that early childhood is the most critical window for learning and development. At Enfance, we foster a child-centric, loving environment that encourages children to ask questions, experiment, build positive habits, and construct knowledge through active discovery.

🎯 Our Mission: To deliver an engaging, activity-based foundation that cultivates intellectual curiosity, creativity, and essential life values.
🌈 Our Vision: To raise confident, communicative, and compassionate future citizens who approach learning with joy and excitement.
❤️ Core Values: Child Safety First, Joyful learning, Mutual Care & Respect, Creative Expression, and Parental Integration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from parents regarding our preschool facilities and admission process.

We maintain a close teacher-to-child ratio. For Playgroup, it is 1:8, and for Nursery and Kindergarten, it is 1:12. This ensures personalized attention and care for every learner.

Our timings are: Playgroup & Nursery: 9:00 AM to 11:30 AM (Monday to Friday). Junior & Senior KG: 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM (Monday to Saturday).

Classrooms and toys are sanitized daily. Our campus has restricted visitor entry, continuous CCTV supervision, and all child spaces are fully cushioned to prevent slips or injuries.

Yes, we offer comfortable van/bus pick-and-drop facilities covering major residential sectors in Ahmedabad. Every vehicle is supervised by a female assistant and has active GPS tracking.
